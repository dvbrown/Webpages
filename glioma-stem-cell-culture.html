Protocol for the culture of Glioblastoma Multiforme stem cells

The cells normally grow as neurospheres in suspension.

    You will need:
  • DMEM/F12 media with Pen/Strep = wash media
  • DMEM/F12 media with B27, FGF, EGF and Pen/Strep = growth media
  • 2% FCS in media = inactivation media
  • 40uM cell filter
  • Ultra-low adherence plates
  • B-27 supplement, without vitamin A
  • Human FGF-2
  • Human EGF
  • 0.05% trypsin/EDTA
  • As an alternative to trypsin use Accutase as it is more gentle
    To reconstitute EGF and FGF:
  1. Both growth factors are used at 10ng/uL final conc
  2. Make 1mL in sterile deionized water = 100ug/mL
  3. From this stock solution make 20x 50uL aliquots
  4. When you are ready to make media dilute 50uL aliquot in 450uL DMEM/F12 + 2% FCS
  5. Add to media bottle = 1:10,000 from original stock
    Dissociation of cells:
  1. Draw up neurospheres with pipette and dispense into appropriate falcon tube
  2. Spin down tube in centrifuge 1,000 xg, 5min @ room temp
  3. Aspirate supernatant and resuspend in approx 2-4mL of 0.05% trypsin/EDTA and incubate at 37 degrees for 5 min
  4. Resuspend cells with plastic bulb (pasteur) pipette and return to incubate for an additional 5 min
  5. Add double the amount of inactivation media to inactivate trypsin (total vol 10mL) and spin down cells
  6. For accutase, inactivation is by dilution, so no FCS is necessary.
  7. Resuspend cells in wash media and spin again
  8. Repeat washing step for a total of 2 washes
  9. Resuspend cells in growth media and filter through 40um mesh into a 50mL falcon tube
  10. Count cells if appropriate
  11. Dilute cells to required volume and plate into ultra-low adherence plates
