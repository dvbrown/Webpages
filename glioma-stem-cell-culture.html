Protocol for the culture of Patient Derived Glioblastoma Cells (PDGCs)

The cells normally grow as neurospheres in suspension. I typically make 250mL of cell culture media and use the other 250mL as a wash media

Materials

  • DMEM/F12 media with only Pen/Strep = wash media
  • DMEM/F12 media with B27, FGF, EGF and Pen/Strep = growth media
  • 40uM cell filter
  • 6 well Corning ultra-low adherence plates
  • B-27 supplement, without vitamin A.
  • Human FGF-2
  • Human EGF.
  • 1 x Accutase
    To reconstitute EGF and FGF:
  1. Both growth factors are used at 10ng/uL final conc
  2. Make 1mL in sterile deionized water = 100ug/mL
  3. From this stock solution make 20x 50uL aliquots
  4. When you are ready to make media dilute 50uL aliquot in 450uL DMEM/F12 + 2% FCS
  5. Add to media bottle = 1:10,000 from original stock

Dissociation of cells:

  1. Draw up neurospheres with pipette and dispense into appropriate falcon tube.
  2. To maximise retrieval of cells pipette 1mL of wash media into empty well and take out remaining cells.
  3. Spin down tube in centrifuge 500 xg, 5min @ room temp
  4. Aspirate supernatant and resuspend in approx 200uL- 1mL of accutase. TYhe amount of accutase to use is determined by how many cells you have.Incubate at 37 degrees for 5 min
  5. Resuspend cells with a 1mL pipette and hold the tube under a microscope at 5x magnification. You should be able to see a cell suspension or clumps of cells. If there are still clumps, return to 37 degrees for an additional 5 min
  6. For accutase, inactivation is by dilution. Resuspend cells in 10mL of wash media and spin at 500 g.
  7. Resuspend cells in the appropriate amount of growth media to plate out. If a single cell suspension is required (eg FACS) filter through 40um mesh into a 50mL falcon tube.
  8. Count cells if appropriate.
  9. Dilute cells to required volume and plate into 6, 24 or 96 well ultra-low adherence plates
