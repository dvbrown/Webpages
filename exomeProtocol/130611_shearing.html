Procedure for the production of exomic DNA libraries for Illumina sequencing

You will need the following consumables

  • 1ug of high quality genomic DNA per sample
  • NEBnext DNA library preparation kit for Illumina. Only need up to adaptor ligation
    1. Illumina adaptors 1 - 12 (kit A)
  • Qiaquick PCR clean up columns or equivalent
  • 550uL Recommended, may change for size selection AMpure XP beads. Bring to room temperature before use
  • Fresh 80% ethanol for use with the beads
  • SeqCap EZ reagent kit. This contains human COT DNA, primers, PCR master mix and hybridisation/ wash buffers.
  • Dynabeads M-270 strepavidin beads for library capture
  • qPCR reagents for enrichment quantification
    1. At least 4 primer pairs for NSC control regions. Consider ordering custom regions.
  • PCR grade water
