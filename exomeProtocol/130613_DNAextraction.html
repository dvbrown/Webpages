Genomic DNA extraction from cell pellets or whole blood

Materials

  • Cell pellet or frozen vial
  • Alternatively 200uL of whole blood that has had 1.5mg EDTA:1mL blood or 2% v/v added
  • Nucleospin DNA blood kit
  • Proteinase K which comes with the kit. Stored in -20 C
  • RNase that was sourced from CTP lab (may buy some)
  • PBS -/- for resuspending/ washing cell samples
  • 100% EtOH
  • Heat block set to 56 C
  • Timer for incubations
  • PCR grade water

Method.........Date completed...........................................................................

Thaw cell pellets on ice. Equilibriate whole blood to room temperature. Heat 100uL of elution buffer per sample.

  1. Resuspend cell pellet in 200uL of PBS or take a maximum of 200uL of whole blood
  2. Add 25 uL of proteinase K
  3. Add 200uL of buffer B3 to the sample and vortex vigourously.
  4. Add of xuL RNase.
  5. Incubate the samples at 56 C for 1 hour, vortexing every few minutes. The lysate should turn brown.
  6. Add 210uL of 100% ethanol to the sample and vortex.
  7. Load the sample into the binding column. For increased efficiency spin at 2000rpm for 1 minute before a faster spin at 12,000rpm.
  8. Replace the collection tube and wash with 500uL buffer BW then B5.
  9. Dry the membrane by spinning again.
  10. Perform the most efficient elution by adding 50uL of heated elution buffer, incubate for 3min and spin. Repeat with another 50uL of buffer and repeat.
    11. Can perform an option ethanol precipitation step to remove any traces of RNase. Ethanol precipitation

Proceed to shearing DNA Shearing using the covaris
