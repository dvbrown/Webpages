Materials

  • Complete media for sample collection
  • HBSS buffer for FACS. Store this buffer cold
    1. 1x Hank's Balanced Salt Solution (HBSS) - 49.5mL
    2. 1% Fetal Calf Serum (FCS) - 500uL
    3. DNase I
    4. DAPI - add just before sort
  • 45uM cell strainer
  • FACS tubes
  • Accutase
  • Wash buffer - DMEM/F12 + Pen/ Strep
  • CD133-APC conjugate antibody (clone AC133 #130-098-829)
  • CD15_PE (SSEA-1) conjugate antibody (clone MC-480)
  • CD44-FITC conjugate antibody (clone DB105 #130-098-210)
  • Isotype control Keyhole limpet hemocyanin (not expressed in humans) BD Bio340442

Procedure for neurosphere dissociation

Work quickly and on ice to maximise viability. This procedure can be performed on the bench.

  1. Pipette cells from dishes into appropriate sized falcon tube. Rinse empty wells with some wash buffer and add to tube.
  2. Spin at 1000xg for 5 minutes in a centrifuge at 4째C.
  3. Resuspend in 5mL wash buffer and spin again.
  4. Resuspend in 500uL - 3mL accutase as appropriate for cell number
  5. Incubate at 37 째C for 2x 5 minutes, resuspending cells with a pipette in between.
  6. Dilute accutase with 4mL FACS buffer (cold). Put through 45uM cell strainer into a fresh 50mL tube.
    Pipette some FACS buffer first to wet the membrane. Transfer 5mL of cells to a FACS tube.
  7. Transfer approximately 10% (500uL) of this cell suspension to another FACS tube to be the isotype control.
  8. For the sample with most cells, take an additional 3 tubes for single stain controls.
  9. Take 50uL for a trypan blue count and record cell number on sample sheet.

Antibody labelling

  1. Spin down at 500xg at 4째C for 5 minutes.
  2. Resuspend in 90uL for 100uL final vol. Add 5uL (1:21) each antibody or isotype control, depending on the sample.
  3. Incubate for 20 minutes in the dark in the fridge (dark).
  4. Wash with 1mL of FACS buffer and centrifuge for 500xg at 4째C for 5 minutes.
  5. Resuspend in a sufficient amount minimum 300uL of FACS buffer for FACS sorting. Aim for 5 - 10 million cells per mL.
  6. Before sorting add 1:100 of DAPI to each tube.

Analysis with flowJo software

Use the single stain samples to gate a positive and negative fraction for each stain. Click on the compensation matrix wizard and follow the prompts. Fluorescence compensation is the mathematical subtraction of the fluorescence due to one fluorochrome from the fluorescence due to another fluorochrome. This is necessary because emission spectra from two fluorochromes can overlap.
