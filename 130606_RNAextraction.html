Protocol for RNA extraction

Materials and methods

You will need

  • At least least 50,000 cells according to the original NEBnext RNA-seq library prep kit
  • Trizol
  • Chloroform
  • Molecular grade ethanol
  • Machery Nagel RNA XS clean up kit
  • Molecular grade water

The procedure couples the extraction of crude RNA/ DNA from a phenol/ chloroform extraction (Trizol) and cleans up the reaction using a Machery Nagel kit.

Procedure

  1. Resuspend the frozen pellet in 500uL Trizol (depending on cell number). Pipette up and down and incubate for 5 minutes.
  2. Add 1/5th volume of chloroform. ie 100uL. Shake vigourously and incubate for 3 min at room temp.
  3. Spin for 15 minutes at maximum speed. The upper phase (RNA) is about 50% of the volume.
  4. Remove the aqueous phase of the sample by angling the tube at 45° and pipetting the solution out. Avoid drawing any of the interphase or organic layer into the pipette when removing the aqueous phase.
  5. DNase treatment of the crude lysate
    • Prepare enzyme-buffer premix: Add 3 μL rDNase to 27 μL Reaction Buffer for rDNase per sample.
    • Add 1/10 volume of enzyme-buffer premix to the crude RNA extract (e.g. to 300 μL RNA extract add 30 μL of the premix comprising buffer and enzyme).
    • Incubate for 10 min at 37 °C
  6. Add 1 volume of buffer RCU (330uL) from the RNA XS clean up kit
  7. Load the RNA XS column twice with 315uL of extract to avoid overloading the column.
  8. Wash with 400uL of buffer RA3, spin and then a second wash with 200uL of RA3.
  9. Elute with the maximum of 30uL of distilled water provided in the kit.
  10. Make a 1:5 dilution and quantify using the Qubit in DNA extraction lab of CTP lab.
  11. Use the diluted sample for a RNA pico bioanalyser run in the range of 200 - 5000pg/uL.

Recommended amount of total RNA for the next step 100ng - 1ug. A lower limit of 200ng RNA is advised.

mRNA purification

Date performed..............................................................................................................................................

Use the NEB mRNA magnetic isolation module and start with protocol (A) of the NEBnext mRNA ultra directional kit manual.

  1. Prepare the first strand synthesis buffer and store on ice during the mRNA purification.
  2. Dilute the total RNA with nuclease-free water to a final volume of 50 μl in a nuclease-free 0.2 ml PCR tube and keep on ice.
  3. Prepare the oligo-dT beads
    • Aliquot 15 μl of NEBNext Oligo d(t)25 beads into a nuclease-free 0.2 ml PCR tube.
    • Wash the beads by adding 100 μl of 2x RNA Binding Buffer to the beads. Pipette the entire volume up and down 6 times to mix thoroughly.
    • Place the tubes on the magnetic rack at room temperature for 2 minutes.
    • Remove and discard all of the supernatant from the tube. take care not to disturb the beads.
    • Remove the tubes from the magnetic rack.
    • Repeat the wash step.
  4. Resuspend the beads in 50 μl of 2x RNA binding Buffer and add the 50 μl of total RNA sample from step 1.
  5. Place the tube on a thermal cycler and close the lid. Heat the sample at 65°C for 5 minutes and hold at 4°C.
  6. Remove the tube from the thermal cycler when the temperature reaches 4°C.
  8. Place the tube on the bench and incubate at room temperature for 5 minutes to allow the mRNA to bind to the beads.
  9. Place the tube on the magnetic rack at room temperature for 2 minutes to separate the poly-A mRNA bound to the beads from the solution.
  10. Remove and discard all of the supernatant. take care not to disturb the beads.
  11. Remove the tube from the magnetic rack and wash the beads by adding 200 μl of Wash Buffer to the tube to remove unbound RNA, mix thoroughly.
  12. Place the tube on the magnetic rack at room temperature for 2 minutes then Remove and discard all of the supernatant from the tube.
  13. Remove the tube from the magnetic rack and repeat the wash.
  14. Add 50 μl of elution buffer to each tube. Gently pipette the entire volume up and down 6 times to mix thoroughly.
  15. Place the tube on the thermal cycler. Close the lid and heat the samples at 80°C for 2 minutes, then hold at 25°C to elute the Poly-A mRNA from the beads.
  16. Remove the tube from the thermal cycler when the temperature reaches 25°C.
