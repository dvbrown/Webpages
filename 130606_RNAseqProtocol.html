Procedure for production of cDNA libraries for RNA-seq

This protocol will produce strand specific cDNA libraries with low RNA input for sequencing on the Illumina platform.

You will need (for 6 samples)

  • 50 - 250ng of high quality RNA
  • Actinomycin D. Stock 5ug/uL to 0.1ug/uL.
  • 2x DNA clean up column per sample (eg QIAquick)
  • 85uL (550uL) AMpure XP beads. Bring to room temperature before use
  • 6mL fresh 80% ethanol for use with the beads
  • Unique index primers if you intend on multiplexing.
    The NEB multiplex oligos for Illumina are the same adaptor sequence as the in-house Illumina ones

First strand cDNA synthesis...............................................................................................

Take the prepared mixture from the mRNA purification and shearing Incubate the samples as follows:

  1. 10 min at 25 °C
  2. 15 min at 42 °C
  3. 15 min at 70 °C
  4. Hold at 4 °C
It is not necessary to clean up the reaction afterwards.

2nd strand synthesis.................................................................................................................

  1. Add the following to the first strand synthesis reaction.
    2. Reagent x1 volume (uL) x6 volume + 10% (uL)
    2nd strand synthesis mix with dUTP 8 52.8
    Second strand synthesis enzyme mix 4 24.6
    Nuclease free water 48 316.4
  2. Mix by pipette.
  3. Incubate in a thermocycler for 1 hour at 16 C. The samples can be safely stored at 4 C overnight as cDNA.
  4. Clean up the reaction using the Machery Nagel Gel - PCR clean up kit. Elute in 60uL water.

End repair library..........................................................................................................................................

Use a new PCR tube and incubate in a thermocycler:

Reagent x1 volume (uL) x6 volume + 10% (uL)
End repair reaction buffer 6.5 42.9
End prep repair enzyme mix 3 19.8
cDNA 55.5 NA
  1. 30 minutes at 20 °C
  2. 30 minutes at 65 °C
  3. Hold at 4 °C
Proceed directly to adapter ligation.

Adapter ligation..............................................................................................................................

Use 65uL of the reaction from A tailing and perform in a PCR machine set to 20 C and paused.

Reagent x1 volume (uL) x6 volume + 10% (uL)
Blunt/TA ligase Mix 15 99
NEBnext adapter 1 6.6
Water 2.5 16.5
  1. 15 minutes at 20 °C
  2. Hold at 4 °C
The adapter comes from the NEBnext multiplex oligo kit. The USER excision step is performed during the reaction.

Size selection........................................................................................................................................

Another critical step. Make sure the AmpureXP beads are at room temp and well mixed. Make up 80% ethanol fresh
It is best to conduct size selection in 0.2mL PCR tubes and a ring magnet.

  1. To 83.5uL of the ligation reaction add 16.5uL of nuclease free water to bring the reaction volume to 100uL.
  2. Add 60uL (0.6x) resuspended AMPure XP beads and mix by a vortex. Incubate for 5 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Spin the tube on a tommy and place on a magnetic rack for 5 minutes or until the solution is clear.
    Transfer the supernatant to a clean tube and keep it! Retain the beads only for insurance purposes.
  4. Add 25uL (0.25x) resuspended AMPure XP beads to 160uL of the supernatant. Mix well and incubate for 5 minutes at room temperature.
  5. Spin the tube on a tommy and place on a magnetic rack to separate beads from the supernatant. After 5 minutes or when the solution is clear carefully remove and discard the supernatant. Do not disturb the beads.
  6. Add 200uL of freshly prepared ethanol to the tube while in the magnetic rack. Incubate for 30 sec and then discard the supernatant.
  7. Do another wash
  8. Briefly spin the tube in a tommy and put back into the magnetic rack.
  9. Completely remove the ethanol and air dry beads with the lid open for 10 minutes at room temperature. It is critical not to overdry the beads as they will crack.
  10. Elute the target cDNA from the beads with 25uL of nuclease-free water. Mix well and spin in a tommy.
  11. Without disturbing the beads transfer 20uL of the supernatant to a clean PCR tube. It is critical not to carry any beads over to the PCR enrichment as it will fail. If necessary perform a second magnetic incubation.

USER excision, indexing and PCR enrichment.....................................................................................

Make sure the right tube has the right index added to it!
The cycle number can be between 12 - 15 cycles depending on the input mRNA. 15 cycles is recommended for 10ng mRNA.

Reagent x1 volume (uL) x6 volume + 10% (uL)
USER enzyme 3 99
High fidelity PCR master mix 2x 25 6.6
Universal PCR primer 25uM 1 16.5
Index primer 25uM unique! 1 16.5
Size selected cDNA 20 16.5

PCR cycling conditions.

  1. User digest 15 min @ °37 C
  2. Denature 10 sec @ 98 °C
  3. Cycling
    • Denature 10 sec @ 98 °C
    • Anneal 30 sec @ 65 °C
    • Extend 30 sec @ 72 °C
  4. Final extension 5 min @ 72 °C
  5. Hold 4 °C

Purify the PCR product by AMPure XP beads. Putting the product through a column doesn't remove adapter dimer Elute in 20uL water.

Library QC before sequencing

Measure a 1:4 dilution of the library by Qubit. Then dilute again to 1,000 pM for a bioanalyser run.
NEB recommends diluting each library 1:4 and assessing the size distribution of the library on a high sensitivity chip bioanalyser.
Seqanswers suggests bioanalyser alone is not very quantitative

Appendix: Some tips for when handling beads

  • When opening tube lids on the magnet, hold down the tube so liquid doesn't splash everywhere
  • If the pellet is diffuse, especially during enzymatic clean-up give the tube a jiggle when still in the magnet
  • Suck up supernatant slowly as to not disturb the beads
  • For the final elution it is critical not to contaminate the PCR with beads. It is better to elute twice
  • The volume of beads for size selection is critical. Measure the volume of DNA by pipette. It is more accurate to dial down.
  • Top up the solution to 150uL if required
